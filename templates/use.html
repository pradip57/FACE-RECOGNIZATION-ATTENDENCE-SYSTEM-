Admin Login

1. Logging into the Admin Panel:


  •    Go to the admin panel login page.
  •    Enter your admin username and password.
  •    Click "Login."

2. Adding User Details:


  •    Fill in required info(name,roll,email).
  •    Click Add User.
  •    Automatically, Captures Training Images.

3. Taking Attendance as an Admin:


  •    Click on "Take Attendance" on the dashboard.
  •    Automatically Capture Images and Use 'Esc' key to take Attendence.

4. Viewing User Details:


  •    Locate "User Details" on the dashboard.
  •    View User Information.

5. Taking Attendance as a User:


  •    Click On "Take Attendance" on the Homepage.
  •    Automatically Capture Images and Use 'Esc' key to take Attendence..
